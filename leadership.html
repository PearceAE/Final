Teaching and Technology Philosophy (5150:614) The goal of this project was to evaluate the technology plan for your institution. For this project, I evaluated the technology plan which was tied into the 3 year Strategic Plan for Ursuline College. The grant proposal I completed was for KeyBank and I proposed acquiring several pieces of software and technology equipment upgrades that would help launch the Digital Humanities program at Ursuline College
