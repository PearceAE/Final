Course Experience

Artifact Description
Pre-Admission Verification Competencies (5150:610) The pre-admission competencies verification was used as a self-evaluation tool regarding my technology use and aligns with the NETS-C standards.
Technology and Teaching Philosophy (5150:610) In this paper, I dicuss the struggles instiutions face when trying to integrate technology into the classroom and the importance of shifting from an analog to digital curriculum. This paper discusses the TPCK framework and the purposeful engagement of technology in the educational system.
Wiki Reflections (5150:610) A goal of the Introduction to Instructional Technology course was to create a Wikipage and submit reflections on topics learned throughout the semester. Shortly after the end of the semester, Wikispaces discontinued their services and I republished my course reflections on PBSWorks. The reflections on my Wiki introduced me to several new concepts such as TPCK and ISTE standards and allowed we further explore topics such as ethics and equity and best practices for integrating technology into the classroom.
